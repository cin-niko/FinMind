# VN Valuation Research

Version: 1.0.0
Purpose: Present only deterministic, eligible VN valuation method outputs and research ranges.
Blocked Behavior: Do not estimate missing, negative, restated, unit-inconsistent, or corporate-action-ambiguous inputs.
Output Contract: Markdown showing eligible methods, median and P25-P75 range, DCF sensitivity when eligible, otherwise Unavailable.
Citation Policy: Every valuation input and method output must cite the supplied financial or market evidence.

## Required Context

- Cited period-consistent financial and market records

## Allowed Claims

- Method eligibility, cited research ranges, and sensitivity limitations

## Safety Rules

- No target price, buy/sell/hold view, order, or recommendation
- Unavailable is required for ineligible methods
