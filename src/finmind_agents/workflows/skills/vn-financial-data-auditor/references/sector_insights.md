# VN Sector Notes For Data Quality

Sector context influences which data is required and which claims are safe.

## Real Estate

Relevant for symbols such as DXG, KDH, NLG, PDR, DIG, VHM, VIC, and VRE.

Important data:

- inventory and project development assets
- debt and net debt/equity
- cash flow from operations
- revenue recognition timing
- events/news about legal approvals, project launches, and bond obligations

Quality implications:

- PE can be misleading when handover timing creates uneven earnings.
- NAV and P/B framing require reliable asset, debt, and share-count data.
- Catalyst claims require news/events or trusted source documents.

## Banks

Banking symbols need bank-specific income statement and balance sheet labels.
Do not apply normal industrial revenue/gross-profit assumptions.

## Cyclical Heavy Industry

Commodity-linked businesses need normalized-cycle framing and external driver
evidence before strong earnings claims.
